Welcome to Roy Restaurant

Indian Cuisine

Indulge in the rich and vibrant flavors of India with our exquisite Indian cuisine. From aromatic curries to succulent tandoori delights, our chefs masterfully craft each dish using traditional recipes and handpicked spices. Whether you're craving the iconic butter chicken, fragrant biryanis, or mouthwatering vegetarian options, our Indian menu promises to take your taste buds on a tantalizing journey through the diverse culinary traditions of India.

Mughlai Delights

Experience the opulence of Mughlai cuisine, known for its royal heritage and intricate flavors. Our Mughlai dishes are prepared with utmost care and precision, ensuring a regal dining experience. From tender kebabs to flavorful biryanis, our Mughlai specialties showcase the perfect blend of spices and techniques, transporting you to the grand kitchens of the Mughal emperors.

Chinese Delicacies

Embark on a gastronomic adventure with our tantalizing selection of Chinese delicacies. Our skilled chefs craft each dish with precision, using fresh ingredients and traditional cooking methods. Whether you're in the mood for delectable dim sum, flavorful stir-fries, or comforting noodles, our Chinese menu offers a wide range of options to satisfy your cravings.

Desserts and Beverages

No meal is complete without a sweet ending, and at Roy Restaurant, we offer an extensive dessert menu to satiate your sweet tooth. Indulge in our delightful selection of traditional Indian sweets, luscious desserts, and refreshing ice creams that will leave you craving for more. Complement your meal with our range of beverages, including refreshing mocktails, classic cocktails, and a variety of hot and cold beverages.

Our Commitment

At Roy Restaurant, we are committed to providing you with the highest quality of food and service. Our skilled and dedicated team strives to create a warm and welcoming environment where you can relax, savor the flavors, and create lasting memories with your loved ones. We source the finest ingredients, maintain strict hygiene standards, and prioritize customer satisfaction above all else.
